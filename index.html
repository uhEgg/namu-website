Our Mission

Young people are already changing their communities — quietly, with little recognition. Namu exists to make sure they're remembered.
🧭 🍃 👣 🍃 👣

🌱

Recognize

We feature young changemakers on social media so their work gets seen — proof of how much youth are already tackling the problems in their own communities.

02
🌍

Connect

Our international fellowship links student changemakers across Namu USA, Namu Indonesia, and Namu Korea — weekly meetings turn distance into real conversation.

On Instagram, @namuorg shows the many ways students volunteer around the world, and the issues this generation actually cares about.
